All about our boarding

When we began Pets_R-Us more than a decade ago, we first only had the idea of selling pet products for our local community. As time went on many people including our friends started to ask us if we would ever consider boarding services. We always had the space for it, but never gave it any thoughts. My wife Marilyn Monroe of 30 years was one who was very excited about the idea. The only problem was we were getting to retirement age and would need to find someone who would be willing to come in night and weekends to attend to the animals since we worked the store all day.

Ten years after opening our doors we decided to make our Pets-R-Us a reality. We currently have sixteen workers who are available to your pets needs around the clock. Our facilities are all climate controlled and we also have the latest security alarms from Turvanti Securities. The security systems include 24/7 surveillance of your pet so you can see there every move regardless where in the world you our. During your pets stay, we feed the twice a day. Once in the early morning and once in the late evenings. If you have any preference on the times you want your pets fed, we can definitely accommodate those request. We also have recreation time for the three times a day Monday through Friday and twice a day Saturday and Sundays.
