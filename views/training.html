Meet the trainer

Meet are Pet-R-Us trainer Al. Al started as a movie actor in Pittsburgh Pennsylvania in 1965. During his years of making movies Al always fell in love with the idea of taking care of his pets while on the road. Many times between sets he would go to the local pet store and volunteer to walk the pets within the local community. Shortly after several movies which included Starky and Hutch the tale of two dogs, he was committed to dedicated the rest of his career to training dogs. Soon after his last movie Batman Dies again, Al decided to move to his home town of Omaha Nebraska, where he began to volunteer his time at the local pet shelter. While he was working at the shelter he met his long time friend and dog whisperer Cesar Milon. Cesar did not hesitate to teach Al the very following day everything he learned throughout his career as a dog whisperer. We are proud and happy to announce that starting two weeks ago, Al decided to come work for Pet-R-Us full time.
